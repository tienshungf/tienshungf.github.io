Escape my Room allows you to play the Escape Room game at your own home. All you need to do is download our rules and printouts, and the app "Zappars". Using augmented reality technology, each custom printout will unlock clues in your own home that will allow you to play until you win the game. This lets you play the popular group game without the hassle of planning, booking, and paying!
